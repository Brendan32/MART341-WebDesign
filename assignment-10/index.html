Prophesy of the Sunset

The Prophesy

When the Sun sets on the 7th of July, 2023, the last human being on the surface of the Earth will die. Cleansing fires will wash over the planet for seven days prior, leaving only the untainted plants and animals. The only survivors of the human race will be underground. These few will forge new societies, led by the Doctrines of the Fire Kings. The Folk of the Sunset Age will create a new world without war or nation.
