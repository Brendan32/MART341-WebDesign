Why Choose This?

I have been interested in languages and how they work for quite some time. This interest extended pretty quickly into creating my own languages. While I continue to try making a language by writing a whole dictionary and such, I often make little projects like these that are simpler to develop and take less time. I figure it might be interesting for others to learn about, so I figured I would share how I usually make them.
